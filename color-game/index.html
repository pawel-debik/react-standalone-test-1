This is some helpful text. When you go into a game then you see a text bar and a random color next to the text that says: Color: . For example, when the color ball is green, then you have to type in the text bar under the text: green. Then press submit. And light blue is cyan. Beware that there are also timers in race game, levelmode and scoregame but not in practicemode. AND there's also a score counter by the upper right part of the screen.
